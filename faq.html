Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Safe Horizon?

    Safe Horizon is a disaster management platform that provides early warnings, emergency response coordination, and recovery assistance to communities affected by natural and man-made disasters.

  • How does Safe Horizon help during disasters?

    We provide real-time disaster alerts, emergency response plans, resource allocation, and post-disaster support to ensure quick recovery and minimize damage.

  • How can I receive disaster alerts from Safe Horizon?

    You can sign up for our emergency notifications through SMS, email, or our mobile app to receive real-time updates on potential hazards in your area.

  • What types of disasters does Safe Horizon cover?

    We monitor and provide assistance for natural disasters such as earthquakes, floods, hurricanes, wildfires, and tsunamis, as well as human-made crises like industrial accidents and infrastructure failures.

  • How can I prepare for a disaster?

    We offer preparedness guides, emergency checklists, and training resources to help individuals and communities create effective disaster response plans.

  • What should I do during an emergency?

    Follow official instructions from emergency services, use our platform for real-time updates, and access our resources for evacuation procedures and safe shelter locations.

  • Does Safe Horizon provide emergency aid and relief supplies?

    Yes, we coordinate with relief organizations to provide food, medical aid, shelter, and other essential supplies to affected communities.

  • How can I report a disaster or request help?

    You can report emergencies through our website, mobile app, or hotline. Our team ensures quick response and directs the necessary aid to affected areas.

  • How can I contribute to disaster relief efforts?

    You can support relief operations by donating funds, volunteering for emergency response programs, or spreading awareness about disaster preparedness.

  • Can organizations partner with Safe Horizon?

    Yes, we collaborate with government agencies, NGOs, and private organizations to enhance disaster response, resource mobilization, and community resilience programs.
