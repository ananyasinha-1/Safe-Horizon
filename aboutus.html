About Us

Who We Are

Safe Horizon is an innovative disaster management platform designed to enhance preparedness, response, and recovery during natural and man-made disasters. Developed by a team of dedicated first-year college students, our mission is to leverage technology to provide real-time alerts, emergency resources, and AI-driven disaster predictions to help communities stay safe and resilient.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between technology and disaster response by providing an intuitive and reliable platform that ensures timely communication, resource allocation, and public safety during emergencies. Our goal is to empower individuals, communities, and authorities with the tools they need to respond effectively to disasters and mitigate their impact.

What We Do

  • Real-Time Alerts & Warnings– Stay informed with AI-powered disaster predictions and early warnings.
  • Emergency Resources & Assistance– Access vital resources, shelters, and emergency contacts instantly.
  • AI-Driven Risk Assessment– Predict potential disaster impacts using data analytics and machine learning.
  • Community Support & Coordination– Connect with rescue teams, volunteers, and government agencies.
  • Interactive Dashboard– A user-friendly interface for tracking disaster updates and managing crisis response.

Our Vision

We believe that technology has the power to save lives. By integrating AI, real-time data, and community-driven solutions, we envision a future where disaster preparedness is accessible to all, and response efforts are swift and effective.

Who We Are Behind Safe Horizon
